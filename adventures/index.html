THE ADVENTURES...

...The tale of The Blade, from Convention to C0DA.

Glass Crown Brass Tower Falser Dreamer Marble Jaws Riven Throne World Eater Bleeding Sun Starless Sky Dead Moon

Every Aurbis carries a tale, and some carry more than one. Many are abandoned, a few are left finished. All of them, in some small way, are true. In these volumes, I have attempted to record a particular tale; one of a peculiar Dunmer, in a peculiar Aurbis. This is his story.
