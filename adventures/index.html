The Adventures...

Every Aurbis carries a tale, and some carry more than one. Many are abandoned. Few are left finished. All of them, in some small way, are true.

In these volumes, I have attempted to record a particular tale; one of a peculiar Dunmer, in a most peculiar Aurbis.

This is his story.
