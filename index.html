Welcome, Dreamer

Across the turning of kalpas and the ruin of kingdoms, stories are lost.

Some to fire, and others to silence.

I am The Scribe Unmoored: the Law, the Last, and the Dead. Preserver of tales from within the multiplicity of Aurbeal spheres and without. I collect these tales and bring them, dear Dreamer, to you.


Of particular interest to me is the record of a singular Aurbis, long since lost in its revolutions. I found its corpus upon the dusty plain of a dying moon, orbiting a world whose name—though I did not know it at the time—was Nirn.

In its original tongue, it was known as 'The Adventures.'

The remaining pages are many, and their words alien. I ask that you join me on the journey of their dec0ding.


    - Scribe
